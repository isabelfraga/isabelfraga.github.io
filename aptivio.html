Overview

The focus of this project was to enhance the usability and overall user experience of Aptivio’s sales intelligence platform. This involved conducting in-depth user research to better understand the needs, behaviors, and pain points of the target audience. Insights gathered from this research informed the design and implementation of key improvements across the platform.

Additionally, it required a mobile version , enabling users to access essential features and data seamlessly while on the go.

Discover

What is Aptivio's solution?

Aptivio is an AI-powered tool that helps B2B sales teams find high-quality leads by analyzing company intent signals and aligning sales activity with the most promising customers based on the target buyer personas, including app-specific for the user.

Clients Feedback — Problems

Pain Point Client Feedback
Lack of intuitiveness Clients find the software difficult to understand and use without training.
Platform Rejection Some clients prefer to keep leads tracked in their CRM instead of using the platform directly.
Confusing information There is little consistency and important groupings are not clearly explained.
Overwhelming interface The large amount of information on the screen makes navigation feel heavy.

Enhancements & New Features

Improve the experience and better perceived value through a renewed design style and visual system.
Improve button language and design intent through better information hierarchy and calls to action.
Allow clients to make changes on their sales playbook configuration and add new products.
Operational dashboards to track market, campaign, and usage trends in real time.
Provide sales teams with filtered sales opportunities based on relevant signals.
New feature: "My Network" helps users connect with potential prospects through LinkedIn connections.

Conclusion

Key Takeaways

The new design of the app resulted in a much higher user adoption rate when compared to the legacy platform. Users found the new experience more visually appealing and easier to navigate, with clearer structure and more relevant content.

One of the key lessons is the importance of conducting thorough user research and testing to validate design decisions and ensure they align with user needs and expectations. Continuing to iterate and improve on the product is how the product is maintained relevant.
