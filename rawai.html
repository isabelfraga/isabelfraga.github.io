Overview

Raw AI Images is a stock image platform focused on AI-generated visuals. Unlike traditional stock platforms, it creates a dedicated space for creators to upload AI images — allowing others to discover, refine, and reuse them.

Discover

Problem & Objective

Problem

With the rapid growth of generative AI, creators produce large volumes of images — but many are discarded due to minor imperfections. There is currently no platform designed to reuse, refine, or collaborate on these unfinished assets.

Objective

Design a platform that transforms unused AI-generated images into valuable resources by enabling discovery, reuse, and collaborative improvement.

Target Users

👤
The Uploader
AI Image Generator

Generates AI images and wants to share or repurpose unused outputs. Needs an easy way to upload and monetise their work.

👤
The Downloader
Designer & Marketer

Looking for diverse and unique visuals that go beyond traditional stock imagery. Needs quick access to ready-to-use images for their projects.

Define

Solution & Product Decisions

Raw AI Images provides a platform where users can upload, browse, and refine AI-generated images. By combining advanced search, basic editing tools, and community-driven feedback, the platform enables AI images to gain new value.

Key Product Decisions

Search-first experience

The platform prioritizes search as the main interaction, allowing users to quickly find relevant images through filters and AI-powered tagging.

Built-in editing tools

Basic editing features were included to allow users to refine images without leaving the platform, reducing friction in the workflow.

Community-driven system

Ratings and feedback were introduced to encourage collaboration and continuous improvement of images across the platform.

MVP Scope

To focus on core value, the MVP includes:

  • Image upload and management
  • Advanced search and filtering
  • Image download
  • Basic editing tools
  • Rating and feedback system

Differentiation

Unlike platforms such as Unsplash or Shutterstock, which focus on polished final images, Raw AI Images centers around raw, imperfect AI-generated content.

The platform introduces collaboration and refinement as core features, positioning unfinished assets as opportunities rather than waste.

Design

Final Design

The final design focuses on clarity and usability, with a clean interface that prioritizes search, image discovery, and quick interaction. A minimal visual style was chosen to keep the focus on the content while ensuring an intuitive user experience.

Conclusion

Key Takeaways

  • Designing for both creators and consumers required balancing different needs throughout the process.
  • Structured upload flows are essential to guide users without overwhelming them.
  • Prioritizing search and simplicity was key to improving overall usability.
