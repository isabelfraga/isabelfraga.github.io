SPARKLE

Overview

Sparkle is a mobile app meant to optimize the use of solar energy by monitoring energy production and consumption, with a gamification element, where users can earn tokens for learning energy-saving tips and spend them on eco-friendly products.

Discover

Desk Research

Rising energy prices

Energy prices have been rising since 2021 due to a combination of factors such as Russia withholding gas supplies to Europe.

Solar energy boom

Solar power is undergoing a boom, as the energy crisis drives a shift to renewable energy, and is expected to surpass coal power by 2027.

Solar energy limitations

Several factors can affect the energy production of solar panels, such as time of day and the weather. People with solar panels often rely on the electrical grid to meet their energy needs.

Survey — 152 participants

89%

67%

60%

Ideate

Crazy 8

The Crazy 8s method was used to brainstorm ideas for the prototype. This exercise helped explore different directions, figure out the bottom navigation and main screens, and ultimately focus on the best ideas. It provided a much clearer picture of the overall prototype structure and flow.

Crazy 8s — sp-image6.jpg

We agreed on keeping the bottom navigation to a minimum of 3 destinations:

  • 1Home tab with energy tracking information and sun's set and rise times.
  • 2Token tab with ways to earn tokens and deals to spend them.
  • 3Settings tab to manage user preferences and system configuration changes.

User Testing

During user testing, an issue was identified with the tokens screen. Some users found the amount of information overwhelming.

The Earn and Spend tabs were added to differentiate between the options for earning and spending tokens, reducing the information on screen.

Conclusion

Key takeaways

To conclude, the successful completion of this UX design project was driven by gathering user feedback and conducting testing throughout the design process. This iterative approach allowed for early identification and resolution of potential issues, ensuring the final design effectively met the needs of the target audience.
