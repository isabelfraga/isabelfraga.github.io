Overview

From scattered documents to structured audit intelligence

Falendi is a B2B legal and financial audit platform designed for firms that audit large enterprises. Auditors manage a portfolio of client companies — KPMG, Carrefour, Coinbase — and for each client they run multiple audits per year, categorised as Juridiques, Comptables, or Financiers.

The platform guides the auditor through the entire workflow: configuring audit parameters (type, date range, legal thresholds like chiffre d'affaires, effectifs, total bilan actif), uploading the required legal documents (Kbis, Comptes sociaux, Rapports CAC, Actes, RBE, Statuts...), then triggering an AI-powered analysis that processes each document in real time and surfaces a structured output — flagged anomalies, legal article references, capital calculations, and an executive summary.

Challenge
Audit teams were juggling documents across email, spreadsheets, and shared drives. No single tool connected document collection, legal thresholds, and structured output in one place.
Approach
Mapping the full 6-step audit flow end-to-end — from project dashboard to live AI processing — and designing for clarity, trust, and error prevention at each stage.
Outcome
A professional-grade web platform that turns a multi-day audit process into a guided, repeatable workflow with structured, legally-referenced results.
