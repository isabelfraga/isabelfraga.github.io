Overview

The goal of this project was to increase online sales by improving the user experience on Deportes Laser's e-commerce. This involved reorganizing the website's structure for easier product navigation and purchase, and updating its appearance for a more attractive look.

Overview — dl-image2.jpg

Discover

What is Deportes Laser?

Deportes Laser is a specialty store that sells technical mountain equipment and gear for various outdoor activities. While they have both an online store and a physical location, the majority of their sales come from the physical store.

Internal Research

Products

They sell technical mountain equipment for vertical work, targeting sports like mountain climbing, caving, and camping. Their products are divided into hard goods and clothing, with hard goods being their most popular category.

Sales & Delivery

Physical store sales are higher than online, with most online sales being for hard goods. The store offers in-store pickup and home delivery through GLS for online orders.

Promotions

The store offers sales throughout the year to promote slow-moving products and items that have not sold well.

User Research — Survey & Interviews

We surveyed and interviewed people who participate in sports that need the store's equipment. Main reasons not to buy equipment online: store employee opinion is influential, direct contact with the product is preferred, and lack of trust in the website.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths
  • Good online reviews
  • Unique products
  • National sales
  • High-quality photographs
  • Offers and promotions
  • Sponsorship of mountain sports events
  • Presence on partner websites
  • Informative blog
Weaknesses
  • Poorly organized product categories
  • Specific clientele
  • Limited filtering options
  • Payment gateway and checkout process issues
  • Low presence on social media
  • Different inventory in physical store and online
Opportunities
  • Update aesthetics
  • Increase social media presence
  • Improve website usability
  • Promote on social media
  • Increase visibility on partner websites
  • Add website chat
  • Improve product categorization
Threats
  • Large retailers offer a wider range of products and faster shipping
  • Specialized stores are more familiar to consumers
  • Physical stores provide personalized service and recommendations
  • Modern websites are more trustworthy and user-friendly

Card Sorting

To ensure the site map's effectiveness and clarity, two users were tested. The first user suggested adding backpacks to both the accessories and mountain categories, and hats to both accessories and clothing & footwear.

After implementing these changes, the second user correctly placed the cards in the categories and agreed that backpacks should be included in multiple categories.

Conclusion

Key takeaways

This project highlighted the importance of information architecture in e-commerce design. By reorganizing the product categories through card sorting and user testing, and updating the visual identity to feel more modern and trustworthy, the redesigned Deportes Laser website provides a significantly improved shopping experience that better reflects the quality of the products it sells.
